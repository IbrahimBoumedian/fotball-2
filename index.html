What is Football?

Football, also widely recognized as soccer, is an immensely popular sport played by teams throughout the world. It captivates the hearts of millions with its dynamic gameplay, showcasing the skill, strategy, and athleticism of its participants. The essence of football lies in the fierce competition between two teams, as they strive to outwit and outperform each other in their quest to score goals.
